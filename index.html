About Me

Methodology: My approach begins with a general athletic assessment of physical condition to determine capacity for the specific physical activity. All past medical findings known to members are considered, e.g., heart, respiratory, and digestive disorders, as well as nervous, muscular, and musculoskeletal conditions and chronic diseases (e.g., diabetes, blood pressure). My goal is to improve the general mental and physical condition, body shape, and health awareness of all members to alleviate joint and back pain and enhance psychological well-being. My sports services include training for individuals and groups of all ages, holistic body training, all types of gymnastics, soccer, and all other sports. Progress is closely monitored by creating detailed fitness plans for individual member profiles, taking body measurements every two weeks, and coordinating time and nutrition schedules (starting from 5:00 AM).
