About Me

Methodology: My approach begins with a general medical report to determine the appropriate level of physical activity, assessing the heart, respiratory, digestive, nervous, muscular, skeletal, and circulatory systems, while managing chronic conditions like diabetes and high blood pressure. My goal is to achieve excellence in health and body composition, raising health awareness to manage joint, back, and muscle pain, as well as psychological well-being. Operating as a Personal Trainer for individuals, health, soccer, and all sports disciplines, I provide specialized coaching tailored to age and body type. Progress is tracked through detailed member profiles and bi-weekly body measurements, alongside strict 24-hour time and nutrition management schedules starting from 5:00 AM.
